ABOUT

Welcome to HALMAW, the haunted house in San Vicente where nightmares come to life. Once you step inside, prepare to encounter spine-chilling spirits, lost souls, and shadows lurking in every corner. Do you dare to face the horrors that await?

Rumored to be cursed, HALMAW has been abandoned for centuries, with whispers of ghostly figures and eerie sounds that have haunted this place for generations. Each room reveals a new terror, with mysteries that have left even the bravest trembling.

Enter if you dare, but beware—the spirits here have a way of trapping those who wander too far. Are you ready to face your fears? Embrace the darkness and join us… if you’re brave enough to last until the end.
